About Circuit

Circuit is an independent tech news site covering artificial intelligence, startups, cybersecurity, apps, and product reviews. We write for readers who want clear, direct coverage of what's actually changing in technology — without the hype.

Our goal is simple: cover a story well enough that reading it here is more useful than reading five other versions of the same headline. We focus on explaining why something matters, not just that it happened.

What we cover

Our coverage spans five areas: AI tools and research, startup news and founder stories, security and privacy, mobile and desktop apps, and hands-on product reviews. We aim to be useful to both people working in tech and people who just want to stay informed.

Corrections and feedback

If you spot an error in one of our articles, or want to get in touch for any other reason, please visit our Contact page.
